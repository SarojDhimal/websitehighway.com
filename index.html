Who We Are

This is an organization of NGOs working in various fields of social welfare and development. Established in 2076 for the promotion and protection of social justice, human rights and pro-poor development, It has working in the various sectors of health ,education and foods.It is actively working to unite, organize and mobilize civil society and helping the local poor community.
